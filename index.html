ABOUT US

Order for a fresh cake and get delivery anywhere in Nairobi. Our cakes are baked everyday to guarantee freshness and yummiest taste. Our unit of measure is recipe per kilogram. This means that the total weight may vary (Weigh more) but the ingredient used to bake are standardized to a thousand grams. You can place order by browsing to your preferred cake, selecting the size of the cake in Kgs, customize your order by indication what will be written on the cake and cake addons, then add to cart and check out. Alternatively you can place the order on WhatsApp Chat or Call. Our customer support team will take and process your order.
